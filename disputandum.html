EVENTS

Eight exciting events. 3 eventful days. Off-the-charts participation.

Dissension
Parliamentary Debate

Debate tournament with PD 2v2 format includes a 7 min 20 sec individual speaker time with an added 2 min individual interrogation allocated for each participant followed by a 4 min 20 sec reply speech from each side of the house. Other proceedings remain the same as a regular asian parliamentary debate tournament.

Casing Trials
Literary

The Casing Trials is a Case Study event, where students participate in teams of 2-3. The events consists of 3 rounds a preliminary short quiz round, after which all the shortlisted teams will be emailed the case study, and will be required to submit the solution in 48-72 hours. The top 10 submissions would be invited to present their solution document to a panel of judges followed by a 5-minute Question and Answer round.

Prime Ministerial Race
General Debate

The Prime Ministerial Race will be a simulation of the Indian General Elections where participants will form a team of 2-3 members and will be allotted a Party portfolio. They will then draft a manifesto, discuss issues of national importance, form alliances and participate in the general elections.

Conventional Debate
General Debate

We will be holding a general debating event as a part of Disputandum. It will be a conventional debating event of the Turncoat format. Each participant will get 3 minutes, which would be equally divided for the two opposing stances. After three minutes, one out of the other participants will get the chance to ask one question to the current participant, the interrogation shall be one minute long.

Dikotomy
Literary

Dikotomy is a A Turncoat Poem Writing Competition with individual participation. Participants will have to turn in a short poem with consecutive lines being polar opposites in the literal meaning. The genre of the poem written will be left upon the discretion of the participant.

Throw a Wrench
Literary

Throw a Wrench is a 2 round-eliminating type event:
Round A: The teams will be given 6 absolutely random characters, the teams will have to choose any 3 characters and prepare a story. Ten teams will be shortlisted and will move to the next round.
Round B: The teams will be paired, and the teams have to introduce a plot twist in their existing story, based on the keywords picked out for them by the opponent team. A winner will be selected from each pair. The Final Winner and the Runner-up Team will be announced by the Organising team.

Fandemic
Literary

Fandemic is a Graphic Designing Competition with Individual participation. The participants will have to design a poster/digital art crossing two or more Universes or Fandoms. The selection of the fandom(s) includes but not limited to books, movies, tv shows, anime, cartoons, etc.

PARACRONYM
Literary

The participants will each be allotted a word by the organizing team, and they have to submit a paragraph wherein the beginning alphabet of each line should compile to form the word which was assigned to them. The entries will be judged by the department itself, and two winners will be announced on our social media platforms.

PAST HIGHLIGHTS


Debsoc, NSUT has conducted several successful events in the past some of which include-

  • COLLOQIUM


    NSUT's Literary and Debating Festival, Colloquium is Delhi's Biggest Debating Carnival which welcomes over 900 Participants from around the country every year. Colloquium hosts various events ranging from Slam Poetry, Research Paper and Case Study Competitions, to Conventional Debates, Model United Nations Conferences, and Parliamentary Debating. Colloquium is undoubtedly the ultimate test of Debating!

  • NSUTMUN


    The biggest model UN Conference in the Delhi Circuit. With hand-picked committees thought after intense deliberations, over 350 Delegates, and an esteemed Executive Board Panel, the conference is the home for a plethora of discussions ranging from Indian Politics to the International Humanitarian Conditions.

  • NSMPD


    One of the most reputed Parliamentary Debating Tournament in the Delhi Circuit. With a Team Cap of 32 Teams, and over 150 participants, NSMPD witnesses the best of Delhi Circuit debating upon a plethora of topics, the debate is a beautiful profusion of rationale, logic and public speaking.

  • IN CASE YOU CAN


    This Case Study Competition allows for participants to deliberate and analyse rigorously selected Cases, which tickle the entrepreneurial mind and tests your business acumen. Participants require the correct blend of logic, rigour and academic brilliance to ace the competition.

  • INQUEST


    Our Research Paper Competition is a theme based competition which allows participants to research, and formulate the unconventional. The Competition witnesses over 100 Registrations annually, and is one of the few notable Research Paper Competition in the Delhi Circuit.

  • RHYME TO FIVE


    Our Poetry Competition allows the participants to create a rhythmic amalgamation, and is the ideal platform for Poetry Aficionados!

  • NSMCD


    This is arguably one of the most looked for events by our participants. The Event explores a wide variety of Conventional Debating Format ranging from Turncoats, to General Debates, to Extempore Competitions. The Event witnesses over 100 participants annually.
