MUN DEPT.

“We are not developing nations, we are not developed nations, we are the United Nations."

  • Advocates of Peace

    DebSoc NSUT attends Model UN conferences throughout the academic year and has innumerable awards to its name.

    With multiple executive board level members of the circuit being a part of DebSoc NSUT, the MUN dept is one of the strongest competitive teams of any kind at NSUT. Being adjudged as the best delegation in over half the conferences it attends, the MUN dept. provides one with a chance to not only experience moments of victory but to organize conferences, learning to work with your peers, juniors and seniors alike, voicing your opinions, moulding and adapting ideas, analyzing, strategizing and optimizing the solutions to problems.

  • Advocates of Peace

    DebSoc NSUT attends Model UN conferences throughout the academic year and has innumerable awards to its name.

    Last season itself, the MUN Delegation of DebSoc NSUT was awarded 69 individual accolades and 5 Best Delegations laurels. And thus, in a small way, our MUN department fulfils aims and goals set out by the founders of the United Nations in the Preamble of the Charter: "To practice tolerance and live together in peace with one another as good neighbours."
