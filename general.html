  • The General Department

    Every week we organise TaD, where hundreds of students show up to try and learn new styles and formats of debating and improve their speaking skills as well as become more comfortable while speaking in front of larger audiences. The General Dept. is also responsible for organising TRAVIAN each year where freshers get to debate amongst each other in an intra-college festival and develop their ability to work in teams and gain a competitive edge as they participate in a variety of events, testing different skills.
