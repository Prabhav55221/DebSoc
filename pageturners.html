The Page Turners

We’re a group of readers, thinkers and writers. So open a book, and turn the page!

  • The Literary Enthusiasts

    The Page Turners was founded in 2015, as a group of people enthusiastic about reading and writing. It has grown to be an integral part of DebSoc, with some of the most popular events during Colloquium under its ambit. TPT maintains its online presence through its Facebook page, where it conducts regular activities and maintains a stream of consistent, diverse literary content.

  • The Literary Enthusiasts

    Despite its short tenure, The Page Turners has managed to hold some successful and convivial events that have been very well received by the students. One such example was the Slam Poetry at Colloquium that saw a huge number of people taking part to show off their poetry skills. Another event that went on to become one of the most popular and interactive events of all time, was the Secret Santa, an online event where people got a chance to draw similarities between fictional characters and their friends. In addition to all the fun events, The Page Turners holds regular book exchanges which gives people a chance to explore new books and try out different genres.
