Who Are We?

Founded in 2008, the Debating Society of NSUT is the premier debating and literary society on campus for one of India's most prestigious and coveted technical institutions, Netaji Subhas University of Technology and is one of North India's most successful debating teams with several annual accolades not just in the North Indian debating circuits but beyond the borders of India as well.

What do we do?

With an aim to promote healthy discussion and careful deliberation, the Debating Society of NSUT provides people with a platform to shed their inhibitions and learn the art of expressing themselves. One of the most renowned debating societies in the DU circuit, it hosts literary and debating events all throughout the year that help students gain confidence and learn the self-empowering skill of public speaking.

Join Us!

Do you question conventional wisdom? Do you wish to break the borders of logic and reasoning? Then this is just the place for you. Come be a part of the Debating Society of NSUT and form your own critical opinion on all the things you've ever believed in. We're searching for people who can look beyond the regular ideals of reasoning, engage in critical thinking and discuss controversial issues without descending to insults.
